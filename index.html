About The Environment Club

The Environment Club of Mengo Senior School is a student-led body dedicated to preserving, protecting and improving our environment both within the school and in the wider community.

We serve as a platform for students to develop environmental awareness, practice sustainable habits, and become ambassadors of green change. We also seek to: promote environmental education by sensitising students on environmental-related issues, encourage sustainable action through environmental sustaining projects, foster student leadership and innovation, and build partnerships with other clubs.
We believe that every small action counts, and together, we can build a future where nature and humanity thrive in harmony.
